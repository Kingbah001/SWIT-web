About SWIT

SWIT (Southwest Innovation & Technology Co.) is the innovation arm of Odu’a Investment Company, backed by the six Southwestern states of Nigeria. We power the region’s transformation by connecting researchers, entrepreneurs, corporates, and government to turn innovation into sustainable economic value.

Our Mission

To foster innovation, support startups, and build a thriving tech ecosystem in Southwest Nigeria and beyond.

Our Vision

A world-class innovation hub positioning Southwest Nigeria as Africa’s leading tech destination.
